<div class="section" id="research">
  <h2>Research</h2>

  <div class="grid">
    <div class="item span-6">
      <h3>OS stack for disaggregated & serverless architectures</h3>
      <p>Remote/disaggregated memory, serverless analytics, consistency, and accelerator-aware data paths.</p>
      <p style="margin-top:8px">
        Recent projects include Spirit (SOSP’25), Scalable Far Memory (SOSP’25), CORD (ISCA’25), and PULSE (ASPLOS’25).
      </p>
    </div>

    <div class="item span-6">
      <h3>Secure cloud systems</h3>
      <p>Oblivious analytics, encrypted data stores, and side-channel defenses for cloud services.</p>
      <p style="margin-top:8px">
        Recent projects include Weave (OSDI’25), Found in Translation (USENIX Security’25), and Pancake (USENIX Security’20).
      </p>
    </div>

    <div class="item span-6">
      <h3>High-performance storage stacks for automated data</h3>
      <p>Compressed storage, multi-attribute data stores, and accelerator-rich systems for emerging applications.</p>
      <p style="margin-top:8px">
        Recent projects include Trinity (EuroSys’24) and SCALO (ISCA’23).
      </p>
    </div>

    <div class="item span-6">
      <h3>Queries on compressed data (prior work)</h3>
      <p>Succinct representations and query processing directly over compressed formats.</p>
      <p style="margin-top:8px">
        Projects include Succinct (NSDI’15), BlowFish (NSDI’16), and ZipG (SIGMOD’17).
      </p>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>
